# The Appel Inn at the Gathering Space

![Appel Inn Birds Eye](./appelinn.jpg)

Our wedding venue is located at 590 Route 146 Altamont, New York 12009, just outside of Albany.

Accommodations for the Appel Inn are being handled by the Best Western nearby at 1228 Western Ave, Albany, NY 12203.
A free shuttle service will be offered by the Best Western to bring guests to and from the Appel Inn the day of the wedding. You can contact the hotel at (518) 489-2981 for more information.


For more information on the venue, [visit their site](https://www.appelinn.com/weddings-events).
